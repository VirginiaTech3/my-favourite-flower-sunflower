h1>🌻 My Favourite Flower: The Sunflower

In the heart of a colourful garden stands a magnificent sunflower, rising tall like a burst of sunshine. It is believed to be named after the sun because of its large, round face and bright golden petals. The flower stands above many others, drawing every eye and bringing warmth to the garden. Bees and butterflies visit often, helping the plants grow and thrive. Birds rest nearby, filling the air with soft songs, while the sunflower turns everyday life into a lively and joyful scene. Sunflower seeds provide us with healthy snacks and are also used to produce cooking oil They are nutritious, valuable, and beneficial to our health. Because of its beauty and usefulness, the sunflower is truly a special flower.
